Welcome to Cruselia

Cruselia is an AI-powered wildlife monitoring tool designed to assist forest officials, researchers, and conservationists. It provides real-time insights into animal populations and conservation status across different regions.

Get Started

Instead of relying solely on traditional censuses, officials can use Cruselia to track sightings, record locations, and monitor trends efficiently.

The platform helps keep track of endangered, vulnerable, and common species, enabling informed decisions for wildlife protection and habitat management.

Key Benefits
  • Monitor animal populations and conservation status
  • Record and track wildlife sightings easily
  • Reduce time-consuming census operations
  • Support data-driven wildlife management decisions
